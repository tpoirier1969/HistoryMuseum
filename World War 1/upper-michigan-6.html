Funding the War Effort

To finance WWI military operations, the government issued bonds or debt securities. Liberty Bonds were aggressively promoted and popularized. Buying a bond was an act of patriotism. 

Famous artists were asked to make posters. Celebrities of the day made public appearances to encourage purchasing bonds. $21.5 billion was raised, primarily by banks and financial groups. The public was somewhat leery of the coercion and bullying in the name of patriotism.
