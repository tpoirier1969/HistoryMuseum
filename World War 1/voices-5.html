Movement Overseas

On January 2, 1918 the Division moved to the ports of embarkation of Hoboken, Newport News, and New York. On January 13 advance detachments sailed from Hoboken and arrived on January 24th at Brest, France. Between January 23rd and February 18th, large parts of the division sailed to England. Other units of the division arrived in St. Nazaire and Brest in late February and early March. The troops which had landed in England, after a short stay in rest camps proceeded to Le Havre.
