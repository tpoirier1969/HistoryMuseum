Technological Innovations 1914 - 1918

The full fury and horrors of modern warfare will be unleashed on the world during World War I. The war will be the first in which the industrial might and knowledge of the warring nations are harnessed for their efforts. Advancements in the killing technology of high explosives, machine guns, and poison gas will bring slaughter on the battlefields to a level that the world had never know.
Advancements in aircraft designs will allow the killing to be taken to the skies above the battlefields. Submarines will bring a new peril to the seas by hunting and attacking merchant ships as well as warships without warning. Automotive technology in the form of the truck will help keep the armies supplied and will be coupled with killing technology to produce armored fighting vehicles. These technological advancements, born during the First World War, will blossom into their full potential during the Second World War.
