The Alsne Marne Operation
July 30 – August 6, 1918

After earning some combat experience with the French Army, the division moved into the lines on the night of July 29 & 30th near Roncheres. On 31 July the division launched its first attack of the war and captured Cierges. On 1 August in the face of determined resistance by some of the best German units, the division continued the attack and secured les Jomblets. On August 2nd the division attacked and captured Mouline Rav. The division continued its advance and by 6 August captured Fismes. During seven days of hard fighting, the division advanced 19 kilometers, captured 18 villages or fortified farms, and defeated the 4th Prussian Guards and 200th and 216th Divisions. During this battle, the soldiers of the division so impressed their French comrades with their aggressiveness the French bestowed on the division a new nickname, "Les Terribles." The division earned their new honor with 777 killed, 3162 wounded and 20 missing or captured.
