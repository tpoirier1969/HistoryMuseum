Albert Hoover "Hard Tack"

These letters were printed in the Portage Lake Mining Gazette and signed “Hard Tack.” The soldier was from Capt. Moody’s company. The writer may be Albert Hoover of Houghton. He enlisted as a private in Company B, 27th Michigan Infantry on August 11, 1862. He was promoted to first lieutenant, Company K and acting assistant commissary of subsistence. He was mustered out with the regiment on July 26, 1865.
