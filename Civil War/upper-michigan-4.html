Jane Masters

Jane was born in Cornwall, England. After marrying, the new couple traveled to New York City. Curious about all the talk of the upper Great Lakes region, they traveled to the region. They lived briefly on Isle Royale where the small group of copper miners ran out of meat in 1852. They moved next to Northwest Mine (Delaware Location in the Keweenaw Peninsula) when the “war of Rebellion” broke out. Jane had a total of 16 children and lived to be 96 years old, and made the Keweenaw her home.
