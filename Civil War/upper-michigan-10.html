USS Michigan Warship in Marquette

The ship returned to Marquette to find the miners back on strike and blocking the rail. The 8th Veteran Reserve Corps was telegraphed, and they arrived in Negaunee by rail on July 15. The strikers were caught between the soldiers on one side and the sailors on the other, enabling the sailors to push through and end the strike.

The three week long strike was ended and as soldiers returned home and flooded the labor market, labor unrest was ended for that time.

Marquette's Lower Harbor, ca 1865
