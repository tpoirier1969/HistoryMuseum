Edward M. Watson

Edward entered the service as corporal in Company B, First Michigan Cavalry. He was wounded in action at Morristown, Tennessee and sent to Michigan to recruit. He returned to serve as lieutenant and captain in Company L, Ninth Cavalry. The J.M. Longyear Research Library has seventy letters written by Edward Watson between 1862 and 1864 to family members in Marquette. Watson uses humor in describing his harsh conditions and the challenges they faced.
