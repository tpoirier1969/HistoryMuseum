First Shooting

“When I stood up for a mark the first time in my life for three fellows to get off from their horses and take aim over a rail fence at me, the first ball passes close to my head, the second fell short and the third struck the fence a little ways off. I hollered to the last one that he couldn't shoot worth shucks. His ball went way to the right. He answered back that he would do better next time, but when I pulled my carbine and sent a bullet in amongst them it would have made you laugh to see how quick they scattered and got on their horses and traveled.”
