Two Ojibwe men from Marquette volunteered for the 1st Michigan Sharpshooters: Thomas Wightman enlisted on Aug. 19, 1863 for three years, age 37. Mustered, Company I, Aug. 28, 1863. Promoted to Corporal. Promoted to Sergeant May 1, 1865. Mustered out at Delany House, DC, May 28, 1865.

Moses Williams enlisted July 4, 1863 for three years, age 30. Mustered, Company K, July 20, 1863. Killed in action July 30, 1863 at  Crater, VA.

 The 1890 Special Census of veterans and their survivors lists “George Madosh Indian.” It gives no service record, but remarks, “I have applied for a Pension but never received any. I am destitute and need help.”
