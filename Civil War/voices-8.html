Captain Moody

“Capt. Moody takes pleasure in acknowledging the receipt of frequent packages of the Gazette, which he distributes among the boys. We are grateful for the favor and return [to] you our hearty thanks. We married men would also tender our sincere thanks to the Supervisors County Clerk, and others interested in obtaining the county bounty due our families in Houghton County, for the promptness and attention they have exhibited in this department. While complaints are made from other sources, I have not heard any from the Houghton boys. The Regiment is very healthy, the medical department is managed with skill by experienced Surgeons.
